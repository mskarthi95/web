About us

KGISL GROUP COMPANIES

The KG Group of Companies is one of the premier industrial groups in India. The KG Group is 70+ year old conglomerate of multiple business divisions with a rich and distinguished heritage based upon interests in information technology, business services, education, healthcare, textiles, finance, engineering, renewable energy and entertainment.

KG Information Systems Private Limited (KGiSL) is a leading global provider of software and business support services and a part of the flagship KG Group of Companies. KGiSL helps clients do business better by leveraging our industry-wide business experience, software expertise and comprehensive portfolio of services. It is the Coimbatore’s first global IT Company. The company reorganized into two units in 2000 to streamline the business and operate more efficiently. Its core units now include.






Management

CEO & Founder

Ashok Bakthavathsalam

Ashok Bakthavathsalam is a successful entrepreneur who has led the growth of Global Software Services (GSS) and Business Support Services (BSS) at KG Information Systems Private Limited. His business acumen is perfectly complemented by the several years of valuable experience in technology management that he gained while working for leading Silicon Valley based high tech companies including Domestic Automation Company, San Carlos, California, USA (now Cellnet Schlumberger) and Hewlett Packard Laboratories, Palo Alto, CA. Ashok Bakthavathsalam was honoured with the prestigious "Doctorate" by Tamil Nadu Agricultural University. He received a Master's degree in Computer Engineering from Syracuse University, NY, and a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from the PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore.

CEO- BSS

Jayamurali Balaguruswamy

Jay is a Senior Executive with over 22 years of accomplished career in Business Development and Sales with over 14 years in Outsourcing, working out of multiple locations in India, Europe and North America. Built the Voice Business Support Services (BSS) team from ground-up since 2004 with focus on Customer Performance, Contract needs, C - Level Relationships and push for improved accountability. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science.

Executive Director

Sridharan Srinivasalu

Sridharan is the Executive Director of KGiSL. He has about three decades of experience in the IT field. He has helped clients offering data processing and software solutions in textiles, engineering, trading, advertising and transportation industry verticals. Sridharan holds a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering and he is involved in Strategic planning, Vision and Direction, establishing core business segments and providing technical and functional support to IT and ITES business units. He has tremendous experience in the working of multiple platforms and technologies to provide cutting edge software solutions to various market segments in the areas of Manufacturing, Healthcare, Finance, Engineering and Transportation.

CEO – Global Software Services

Prassadh S Shanmugam

Prassadh brings extensive experience in IT outsourcing, including software development and Business Support Services (BSS). Vast experience in using various business models including joint ventures, Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT), Build-Operate-Manage (BOM), alliances and partnerships. Prassadh serves as the VP of Coimbatore District Cricket Association (CDCA), one of the premier District Cricket Associations of Tamil Nadu. He holds a Master’s degree in Business Administration.
